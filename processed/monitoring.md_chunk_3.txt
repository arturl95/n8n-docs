## Enable metrics and healthz for self-hosted n8n
<!-- vale on -->
The `/metrics` and `/healthz` endpoints are disabled by default. To enable them, configure your n8n instance:

```shell
# metrics
N8N_METRICS=true
# healthz
QUEUE_HEALTH_CHECK_ACTIVE=true
```
