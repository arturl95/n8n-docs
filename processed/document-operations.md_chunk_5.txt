- **Recalculation Interval**: Enter the desired recalculation interval for the spreadsheet functions. This affects how often `NOW`, `TODAY`, `RAND`, and `RANDBETWEEN` are updated. Select **On Change** for recalculating whenever there is a change in the spreadsheet, **Minute** for recalculating every minute, or **Hour** for recalculating every hour.
